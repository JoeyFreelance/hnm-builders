About HNM Builders Ltd

A strong foundation in craftsmanship

At HNM Builders Ltd, we're dedicated to delivering high-quality construction and building solutions tailored to the unique needs of every client. Built on craftsmanship, reliability and integrity, we take on residential, commercial and renovation projects of all sizes across Woking and the surrounding Surrey area.

We use only premium materials and industry-approved methods — proper foundations, correct drainage, and a flawless finish. Every project is carefully planned and completed with genuine attention to detail, keeping disruption to a minimum and the site clean and safe throughout.

  • Honest advice and clear pricing — no hidden costs
  • Reliable turnaround times, agreed up front
  • Clean, safe sites and minimal disruption
  • Work that adds real, lasting value to your home
